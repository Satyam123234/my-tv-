How to Connect with a TV

A simple guide to connect your devices to a TV.

1. Using HDMI

Connect your device to the TV using an HDMI cable.

  1. Plug one end of the HDMI cable into your device (laptop, gaming console, etc.).
  2. Plug the other end into the HDMI port on your TV.
  3. Switch the TV input to the correct HDMI source.

2. Using Chromecast

Stream content wirelessly using Google Chromecast.

  1. Connect the Chromecast device to your TV's HDMI port.
  2. Set up the Chromecast using the Google Home app on your phone or tablet.
  3. Cast content from your device to the TV.

3. Using Screen Mirroring

Mirror your device's screen to the TV wirelessly.

  1. Ensure both your device and TV support screen mirroring (e.g., Miracast, AirPlay).
  2. Enable screen mirroring on your TV.
  3. Connect your device to the TV using the screen mirroring feature.

4. Using OTT Platforms

Connect and stream content from OTT platforms like Netflix, Hulu, or Amazon Prime Video.

  1. Ensure your TV is connected to the internet (via Wi-Fi or Ethernet).
  2. Download the OTT app from your TV's app store (e.g., Google Play Store, LG Content Store, or Samsung Smart Hub).
  3. Sign in to your OTT account or create a new one if you don't have an account.
  4. Browse and stream your favorite shows and movies.
