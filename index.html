Fast & Easy Setup Instructions for USA

Let's Get You Started

  1. Plug in your TV and turn it on using the power button or remote.
  2. Select your preferred language from the list of options.
  3. Choose the region as United States (USA).
  4. Connect your TV to a stable Wi-Fi network.
  5. Accept the terms and conditions to proceed.
  6. Wait for the system to check for updates and install them if necessary.
  7. Note the activation code displayed on your TV screen.
  8. Open the activation page on your phone, tablet, or computer.
  9. Enter the activation code exactly as shown on your TV.
  10. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the setup.
  11. Once done, your TV will automatically load the home screen.
  12. Sign in with your TV provider or create a new account if needed.
  13. Customize your apps, display settings, and parental controls.
  14. Enjoy your newly activated smart TV!
