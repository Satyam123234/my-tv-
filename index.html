TV Activation & Troubleshooting Tips

Your ultimate guide to setting up and fixing common TV issues

TV Activation Steps

1

Unbox Your TV

Carefully unbox your TV and remove all packaging materials.

2

Connect to Power

Plug your TV into a power outlet and turn it on.

3

Connect to Wi-Fi

Go to the settings menu and connect your TV to your Wi-Fi network.

4

Activate Your TV

Follow the on-screen instructions to activate your TV.

Troubleshooting Tips

!

No Power

Ensure the power cord is securely plugged in and the outlet is working.

!

No Signal

Check all cable connections and ensure the correct input source is selected.

!

Poor Picture Quality

Adjust the picture settings and ensure your TV is receiving a strong signal.

!

Sound Issues

Check the audio settings and ensure the volume is not muted.
